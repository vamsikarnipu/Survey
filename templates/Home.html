Empowering Lives Through Skills

About the initiative

In collaboration with a local NGO, Genpact is on a mission to empower displaced and underserved communities in countries like Bangladesh. These communities, though connected through mobile devices and basic internet access, often lack the skills and confidence to engage with modern technology and employment tools.

This web application provides a simple, accessible interface for individuals of all ages to share their background, interests, and areas where they wish to upskill. The data collected will help design personalised training programmes that enable individuals to become self-reliant and economically independent.

Whether it's learning to cook professionally, start a small business, or improve digital literacy this platform is the first step toward a better future.
